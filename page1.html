Anime, a style of animation originating from Japan, has become a global phenomenon known for its distinctive art, diverse genres, and compelling storytelling. Unlike Western animation, which often targets children, anime caters to a wide range of audiences, including adults. It covers various themes and genres, from action and adventure to romance, horror, and science fiction.

The art style is characterized by vibrant colors, exaggerated facial expressions, and detailed backgrounds. Iconic series like "Naruto," "Dragon Ball," and "Attack on Titan" have garnered massive international followings, contributing to the cultural exchange between Japan and the rest of the world. Beyond entertainment, anime often explores deep philosophical questions and social issues, making it a rich and engaging medium for viewers of all ages.
